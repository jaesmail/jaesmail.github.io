I'm interested in startups, politics, behavior, and talking about the future.

I write Entrepolitical, a newsletter for young and aspiring entrepreneurs and community organizers. My hope is to give readers the tools to leverage and evolve big ideas for their own career development. You can subscribe here.

I founded Palaced, a community for essentially the same demographic where members gather to learn and discuss some of the biggest questions facing the world today. Sign up for our waiting list here.

I led Launch, one of the largest student-run startup incubators in the world. I am also active in various campus activist groups.

I'm studying at Northwestern University where I've been:

  • Executive Vice President of the Muslim-Cultural Student Association
  • a Resident in The Garage
  • a student-researcher at the Farley Center and Kellogg School of Management
  • and involved with various other student groups on campus.
